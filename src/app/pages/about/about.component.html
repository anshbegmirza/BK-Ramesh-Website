About BK Ramesh

NLP & Life Coach · Value Based Living

In the journey of his life, BK Ramesh interacted with a wide variety of people and witnessed many ups and downs alongside them. After becoming part of Brahma Kumaris, a spiritual organization, he felt called to serve people across all walks of life. That is when he discovered NLP (Neuro-Linguistic Programming).

For the last 7 years, BK Ramesh has used NLP to help people transform their lives. He has seen firsthand how many people struggle today — with goal setting, career growth, depression, anxiety, and unhealthy relationships. What he loves most is bringing a smile back to people's faces.

Many of his clients return to complete his full NLP course. He has also developed one of his most loved programs — Personality Development Training for the Perfect Woman — helping women build confidence while understanding the true value of their lives.
